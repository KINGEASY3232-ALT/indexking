Ekusutashi

Artist: IDERA

Price: 5,600,000

Ekusutashi is a beautiful masterpiece, which means "Ecstasy".

painting reflects twin flames: an amalgamation of energies and souls, perfectly complementary as they bring together a trance-like state of pure bliss, reminiscent of the seven heavens with mystic self-transcendence and tons of fire in one physical form.
